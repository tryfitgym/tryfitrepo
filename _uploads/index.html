About New School

“Education is our passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to the people who prepare for it today.” – Malcolm X

The New School is a boarding school located in Chandigarh, India. It lies on the heart of Chandigarh The City Beautiful. General RK Beniwal and Cap. V Batra established this school in 1990 under HQ Western Command. The school is ranked 9th among top boarding schools in India.
The School is affiliated to the CBSE(Central Board of Secondary Education). It is also accredited by International Education Board.
